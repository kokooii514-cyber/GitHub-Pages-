Hash Power Value Formula

$$ V(t) = \sum_{i=1}^{N} \left( H_i(t) \cdot \eta_i \cdot \frac{C_b}{C_i(t)} \cdot e^{-\gamma t} \right) + \lambda \cdot \ln\left(1 + \frac{E_q(t)}{E_0}\right) + \beta \cdot N(t)^\alpha $$

This multidimensional formula dynamically quantifies computing power value, incorporating time decay, emotional value, and network effects.

Image Generation Formula

$$ I(x,y,t) = \mathcal{F}^{-1} \left\{ \sum_{k=1}^{M} \left[ w_k(t) \cdot \delta_k(f_x, f_y) \cdot e^{-2\pi j \cdot (f_x x + f_y y)} \right] \cdot A(f_x, f_y, B(t), E(t)) \cdot G(t, \tau) \right\} $$

Transforms emotional data into visual representations through frequency domain conversion, supporting time evolution and environmental adaptation.
